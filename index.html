FAQ

How do I add new words?

Open a category in the app, then use the add button to create a new word with translation, examples, and optional notes.

Can I edit or delete a word later?

Yes. Open the saved word entry to update its translation, examples, tags, or notes, or remove it if you no longer need it.

Can I import or export my vocabulary?

Yes. VocabHelper supports CSV import and export so you can move vocabulary in or out of the app.

Do I need an account?

You can use core features locally, but signing in is required for cloud sync and some account-based functionality.

How does sync work across devices?

When you are signed in, your supported data can be synced through your account so your vocabulary stays available on multiple devices.

What are AI credits used for?

AI credits are used for AI-powered features such as generated words and some listening or language assistance workflows.

Why is audio or listening not working?

Check your device volume, silent mode, network connection, and any app permissions related to audio playback or speech features.

How does Listening mode work?

Listening mode plays your saved words and phrases so you can review pronunciation, repeat them, and practice more passively.

Why is Listening mode not working?

Check your volume, silent mode, selected language, and network connection. Some speech features may also be limited on certain devices.

How do quizzes work?

Quizzes help you review saved vocabulary by testing recognition and recall of the words you have added to the app.

Can I quiz words from a specific category?

Yes. You can select one or more categories before starting a quiz so the session focuses on the vocabulary you want to practice.

What is Learn New Words?

Learn New Words is an AI-assisted feature that suggests new vocabulary based on your settings, level, and selected interests.

Why can't I generate more words in Learn New Words?

This can happen if you are out of AI credits, temporarily offline, or the request fails because of a temporary service error.

What is speech to text used for?

Speech to text converts spoken input into text for supported voice-driven features and pronunciation-related workflows.

Why is speech to text not working?

Make sure microphone permission is enabled, your device supports the selected language, and your connection is stable if the feature needs online processing.

Why did I reach a speech or text-to-speech limit?

Some speech features depend on device or app usage limits. If you keep hitting the limit, contact support with your device details.

What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?

Force close the app, reopen it, and make sure you are on the latest version. If the issue continues, include the exact action that caused it when you contact support.

How do I request deletion of my account or data?

Send a support request from the email associated with your account and mention that you want your account or stored data deleted.

What should I include in a support email?

Include your device model, OS version, app version, and a short description of the issue so we can reproduce it faster.
